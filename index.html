CHRONOSPACE MOJAVE!

Hello! Assuming you're not here looking for government secrets, you're likely here because you know that the Internet is a bit...weird. It feels corrupt, almost as if something is going on behind the curtains...
My name is Jöel Kinnunen, and I'm on a journey to find out what the big deal is. Everytime I connect to Mojave!, I write about my experiences inside it, all of the goods, all of the bads, and certainly all of the uglies.
If you can stomach it, you can accompany me on my journey by reading it below.


READ CHRONOSPACE MOJAVE! BELOW

  • INITIAL BOOT SEQUENCE: The world is ravaged overnight by a cataclysmic digital event known as "The Big Boom". 2 billion people die as a result.
  • EPISODE ONE: THIS INSTALL IS TAKING FOREVER: Jöel Kinnunen awakens a seemingly innocent Internet suite in search of a safe Internet.
  • EPISODE TWO: WELCOME TO THE INTERNET: Jöel Kinnunen signs onto the "new Internet" for the first time, not finding much of value.
  • EPISODE THREE, PART 1: EVERYTHINGSFINE.TXT: Jöel and Kassie search for the Chronosoft headquarters after sighting concerning imagery online.
  • EPISODE THREE, PART 2: WIN32K_CRITICAL_FAILURE: Jöel must enlist the help of a mysterious stranger to find Kassie and escape the headquarters.
  • EPISODE FOUR: NO MAIN SYSTEM POWER: Jöel and the stranger return to the Chronosoft headquarters to unravel the sadistic mysteries it hides.
