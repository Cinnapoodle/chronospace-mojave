Hello! Assuming you're not here looking for government secrets, you're likely here because you know that the Internet is a bit...weird. It feels corrupt, almost as if something is going on behind the curtains...
My name is Jöel Kinnunen, and I'm on a journey to find out what the big deal is. Everytime I connect to Mojave!, I write about my experiences inside it, all of the goods, all of the bads, and certainly all of the uglies.
If you can stomach it, you can accompany me on my journey by reading it below.
